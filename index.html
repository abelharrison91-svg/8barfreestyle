The Founder

MEET
MAZZA ABZ

Mazza
Abz
// Founder & Creator, 8 Bars Freestyle
UK Creator & Entrepreneur
"There was no short-form content built for rappers. So I went out and built it."

Mazza Abz is a UK creator and entrepreneur who spotted a gap that nobody else was filling. Rappers had the talent, but short-form content on TikTok was dominated by everything except the thing that mattered — raw, unfiltered freestyle culture.

So he did something simple: took rappers to the streets of Central London, hit record, and let them freestyle. What started as 30-second clips quickly grew into one of the most authentic music promotion formats on the platform — and 8 Bars Freestyle was born.

Mazza runs every shoot personally, working directly with artists and labels to make sure every video captures something real. No gimmicks, no filters — just music, streets, and the camera.
